# Review Signals - Technical Documentation

## Category A: Temporal Anomalies
- **Review Burst**: A product receiving 50 reviews in 24 hours after months of silence.
- **Synchronized Posting**: Multiple reviews appearing at the exact same minute.

## Category B: Linguistic Patterns
- **Phrasal Repetition**: Use of the exact same non-standard phrase (e.g., "amazing quality for my family usage") across 5+ accounts.
- **Keyword Stuffing**: Over-optimization for specific search terms within review text.

## Category C: Account Health
- **Single-Item Reviewers**: Accounts created specifically to review one product and then go dormant.
- **Verified Purchase Discrepancy**: High star ratings with no "Verified Purchase" badge vs. low ratings with verified badges.

## Category D: Sentiment Drift
- **Star Rating Gap**: 5-star ratings where the text content is generic or unrelated to the product.
- **Negative Review Pattern**: Evidence of "competitor ghosting" where a sudden influx of 1-star reviews targets a high-performing product.
